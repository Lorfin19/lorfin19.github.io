Story

"After haunting theatres all over North America, the smash-hit musical about everyone’s favorite undead demon comes to its final resting place: Broadway! Based on Tim Burton’s dearly beloved film, Beetlejuice tells the story of Lydia Deetz, a strange and unusual teenager whose whole life changes when she meets a recently deceased couple and a demon with a thing for stripes. With an irreverent book, an astonishing set, and a score that’s out of this Netherworld, Beetlejuice is “A FEAST FOR THE EYES AND SOUL!” (Entertainment Weekly). And under its uproarious surface (six feet under, to be exact), it’s a remarkably touching show about family, love, and making the most of every Day-O! But this strictly limited engagement won’t live forever — the “SCREAMINGLY GOOD FUN!” (Variety) lasts through January 3 only, so book your seats now!" -Broadway.com
