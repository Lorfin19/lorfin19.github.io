Top Game Tips:

When playing games that involve a lot of combat (Minecraft, Halo, etc.), try strafing. According to Wiki, " Strafing is the act of moving sideways in a video game relative to the player's forward direction." This is invaluable when fighting intelligent beings essentially (not NPCs), as it allows you to attack while, hopefully, preventing your opponent from striking you.

While it may seem like a good idea (or at least more interesting) to use WiFi and wireless connectivity technologies for gaming, but that may not be the best thing to do. Ping is the gaming term for the delay between the gamer's input and the server's response to that input. The lower the ping, the faster the response time. It decreases ping to use a wired mouse/keyboard or controller. It also decreases lag to use an ethernet cable instead of wirelessly connecting to WiFi.

Have you ever tried to connect to Hypixel (Minecraft Java) or a server on Minecraft Bedrock and found that you can't connect? This is an easy fix. Simply go to the modem/router in your house, unplug it, and plug it back in. After a few minutes the WiFi will be up and running again and you should be able to connect to any server you want.
