Being a huge theater kid myself, I decided it would be fun to start a YouTube channel where I could post and talk about all my nerdy theater stuff, and since I know there are many others like me, I figured there would be a few people interested in such a channel.

I started Broadway Review because theater isn't always appropriate for everyone, and I believe that people should have the knowledge to be able to make informed desicions about what they or their children should watch and listen to.

My cousin and I created a joint YouTube channel because, throughout our lives, we've come up with some pretty neat stuff that is going to be super fun to chat and start podcasts about.
