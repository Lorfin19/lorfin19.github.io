About OceanCraft:

OceanCraft is a multiplayer Minecraft world owned by me, Lorfinfurball. The state of OceanCraft has parts to it. The capital city, named BeeVille; the quaint little town of Pomegranate City; the city of Polylands, which has a little community called PolyVille and an amusement park called Vacation Island; and the wilderness town of MooseyVille. I, Lorfinfurball, am the former governor of the state of OceanCraft, while the current governor is a good fellow by the name of MooseyKing. Mayor Padow is the, well, mayor of the capital city, BeeVille. DumpTruck20 is the mayor of the amusement park called Vacation Island.
