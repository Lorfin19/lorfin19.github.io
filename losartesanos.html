Welcome to the Official Los Artesanos Webpage!

Here, you can find information about the Minecraft city Los Artesanos.

Government

Currently, Lorfinfurball is the governor and Mayor Padow is the mayor (clearly), her second in command.


Players

A large number of players have joined this world, and besides the government, some frequent city influencers are MooseyKing and DumpTruck20.


Info:

Los Artesanos is a huge world. The city includes a church; a bakery; a large and beautiful garden; and even a theme park; as well as Memorial Lake, which as the name implies is a lake of memorials for incredible people, game-related and not.
