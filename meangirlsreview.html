Content Review

Certain songs contain suggestive lines. Some language as well; two uses of "S***", as well as many rude words, and some talk of underage drinking and drug use. Also a number about dressing "Sexy" on Halloween. One song saying to "Raise your right finger...".

Is it good?

Many fun, upbeat songs. Amazing belting and vocal acrobatics, and talented singers. Each song was written to match its character's personality. The songs range from rock anthems to classic Broadway numbers.
