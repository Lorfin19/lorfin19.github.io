Content Review *for the ORIGINAL CAST RECORDING not the Broadway cast album*

"Don't Lose Ur Head" contains some mild language as well as some suggestive content. "All You Wanna Do" is very suggestive; relationships are the whole idea of the song. Other mildly suggestive lines throughout.

Is it good?

The songs in this musical are very pop-oriented, and most of the numbers are very exciting. Even though some songs discuss heavier topics, it's a fun musical with lots of great numbers.
