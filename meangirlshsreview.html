Content Review: [Individualized]

When licensing Mean Girls High School Edition, you are given the latest full Broadway script (and full songs), witih no edits. It does, however, come with a list of approved changes, leaving the sanitizing up to the individual theater groups. The general storylines do remain the same, however.

Story

After a life in Kenya, 16-year-old Cady Heron moves back to the US and starts school at North Shore High. After befriending two outsiders, who warn her of the trio of mean girls called the Plastics, she soon sees the appeal of being popular and becomes plastic herself.
